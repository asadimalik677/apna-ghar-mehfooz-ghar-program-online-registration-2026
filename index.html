The Problem

Why This Program Was Started

Every year, weak roofs and crumbling houses put thousands of Punjab families at risk - especially during monsoon season. Instead of reacting after accidents happen, the Punjab government decided to take preventive action.

The Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar program helps people strengthen their existing homes before disaster strikes. This is not a typical new house scheme - it is a home-repair and home-safety loan program at its core.

Who Qualifies

Eligibility Criteria

Make sure you meet these conditions before applying. Priority is given to low-income and vulnerable families.

Valid CNIC Required

You must be a Pakistani citizen and permanent resident of Punjab with a valid CNIC.

Proof of Ownership

You must legally own the house or have valid proof of ownership/possession (Registry/Fard).

No Bank Defaults

You must not be a defaulter of any bank or financial institution. A clean financial record is required.

Clean Criminal Record

Applicants should not have a serious criminal record. Background checks are part of the process.

NSER / PMT Score

Registered in NSER with a PMT score of 60% or below. Verify your status before applying.

Low-Income Priority

Priority is given to low-income and vulnerable families. Monthly income typically below Rs 60,000.

Step-by-Step

How to Apply Online

Follow these simple steps to submit your application through the official Punjab government portal.

1

Visit the Official Portal

Go to acag.punjab.gov.pk - the only authorized portal for Apni Chhat Apna Ghar / Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar registration. Do not use third-party websites.

2

Register a New Account

Click "Register" or "Apply Now." Enter your CNIC, mobile number, email, and create a secure password. Use your name exactly as written on your CNIC.

3

Log In & Select Category

Log in with your CNIC and password. Choose either the Rs 500,000 repair loan or the Rs 1,000,000 additional-floor loan category.

4

Fill in Your Details

Enter your personal information, complete address, and describe the condition of your house or roof. Be accurate - incorrect data is the #1 reason for rejection.

5

Upload Documents

Upload clear scans of your CNIC, ownership papers, and house photos. Use JPG or PDF format under 2 MB per file.

6

Submit & Save Tracking ID

Review everything carefully, then submit. You will receive a tracking ID on-screen and via SMS. Use this to check your application status anytime.

Start Your Application

Stay Safe from Scammers

Government housing schemes attract fake websites and fraudsters. Protect yourself and your family:

  • Only use the official government portal acag.punjab.gov.pk to register
  • Never share your CNIC photo, OTP, or bank details with anyone claiming to "process" your application
  • Do not trust unofficial WhatsApp links or social media posts claiming instant approval
  • Approval takes time - the government verifies land records and house condition before releasing funds
  • Applying is completely free. Never pay any agent or middleman
Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about the Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar program.

What is the Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar program?

It is a Punjab government initiative under the Apni Chhat Apna Ghar scheme offering interest-free loans to help families repair unsafe houses or build an additional floor. Approved by CM Maryam Nawaz Sharif in July 2026.

How much money can I get?

Up to Rs 500,000 for house/roof repair and strengthening, or up to Rs 1,000,000 for building an additional floor. Both are 100% interest-free with easy monthly instalments.

Is this only for new houses?

No - this program is specifically for people who already own a house and need to repair or expand it. If you do not own a house, apply for Apni Chhat Apna Ghar instead.

What is the difference between Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar and Apni Chhat Apna Ghar?

Apni Chhat Apna Ghar is for families who do not own a house and need help building one from scratch. Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar is for families who already own a house but need financial help to repair it or add a floor.

Do I need to be NSER/PMT registered?

Yes. Many Punjab welfare and housing schemes use NSER/PMT data to confirm income eligibility. A PMT score of 60% or below is typically required. Verify your NSER status before applying to avoid delays.

How do I check my application status?

Log in to the official portal with your CNIC and password. Go to "Track Application" and enter your tracking ID (received via SMS after submission). You can also call the toll-free helpline 0800-09100.

Is there any fee to apply?

No. Applying through the official government portal is completely free. Never pay any individual, agent, or website that asks for money to "process" or "guarantee" your application.

Can I apply if I live in a rural area?

Yes, the scheme is designed for eligible families across Punjab, both urban and rural. Specific plot-size and property rules may vary slightly by area.

What happens after I submit my application?

Your CNIC, property documents, and income details are verified against government records (including NADRA and NSER/PMT data). Once approved, funds are released in instalments linked to your repair or construction progress.

Who launched this scheme?

The Apna Ghar Mehfooz Ghar scheme was approved by Punjab Chief Minister Maryam Nawaz Sharif during a special housing review meeting in July 2026, as an expansion of the province's flagship housing programs.
